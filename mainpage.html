Our lab investigate and research about computational neuroscience, data mining and machine learning. The goal of our lab is make soild and detail computational neuroscience data set to connect with data mining which is the fundamental of machine learning and develop new method and technology of machine learning in order to build new IOT technology for the future.


Our lab release several science paper for building new method of data mining technology that made interests from several information technology companies. We have worked with them to support thier new technology and we became crucial part for thier project.
